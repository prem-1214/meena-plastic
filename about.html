About Meena Plastic

Delivering quality plastic products for various industries with a commitment to sustainability and customer satisfaction.

Our Journey

Since our inception, Meena Plastics has been dedicated to offering top-quality products like carry bags, garbage bags, and bio-hazardous bags. We pride ourselves on adhering to ISO 9001/2015 standards and delivering innovative solutions tailored to our customers' needs.

  • Wide range of customizable plastic products
